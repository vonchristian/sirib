# Product

## Register

product

## Users

The system serves cooperative banking staff — tellers, loan officers, finance team, management, and compliance officers at Philippine cooperatives. They manage member accounts, process transactions, evaluate loans, reconcile books, and generate reports as part of their daily operations. Many have spent years or decades in manual or disconnected systems; the interface must earn their trust through clarity and reliability, not novelty.

## Product Purpose

Sirib eliminates the repetitive data entry and disconnected systems that slow down cooperative banking. It provides a single source of truth for member accounts, loans, savings, cash sessions, and financial reporting — so that entering data once updates every related surface. The goal is to give staff back time they currently spend reconciling mismatched records and re-entering the same information across multiple tools.

## Brand Personality

Trustworthy, warm, efficient. Professional without being cold — like a capable, trusted colleague who makes everyone's work easier.

## Anti-references

- **No corporate banking aesthetics.** Avoid navy blue, gold accents, marble textures, or any design language that mimics traditional commercial banks. This is a cooperative platform, not a corporate one.
- **No generic SaaS dashboard tropes.** Avoid the cold, utility-only feel of Salesforce, generic admin panels, or tools that feel designed for data-entry operators rather than people with professional judgment.

## Design Principles

1. **One source of truth, always.** A single transaction updates every related surface — member balance, cash position, journal entry, daily totals. No duplicate entry, no reconciliation chase.

2. **For the people who do the work.** Design for tellers, loan officers, and finance staff first. The interface earns its keep by making daily operations faster, clearer, and less error-prone — not by looking impressive in a demo.

3. **Calm confidence.** Banking-grade reliability without corporate stiffness. Trustworthy is quiet, not loud. The system inspires confidence through predictability and clarity, not through visual statements.

4. **Progressive comfort.** Meet users where they are. Familiar workflow patterns, forgiving interactions, generous targets for aging eyes. No forced transitions — let users adopt at their own pace.

5. **Audit-ready by default.** Every action leaves a traceable trail. The surface exposes what matters for the task at hand and tucks away what doesn't. Nothing is hidden from someone who needs to verify.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA compliance as a baseline. Beyond that, consider that some cooperative staff may be older, have limited prior experience with digital tools, or work under variable lighting conditions (bright teller windows, dim back offices). Text contrast, touch target sizes, and forgiving interaction patterns matter beyond the spec.
